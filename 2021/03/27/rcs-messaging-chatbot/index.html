RCS Messaging Chatbot

Huan Li Follow Mar 27, 2021 · 1 min read

Rich Communication Services (RCS) is a communication protocol between mobile-telephone carriers and between phone and carrier, aiming at replacing SMS messages with a text-message system that is richer, provides phonebook polling (for service discovery), and can transmit in-call multimedia. It is part of the broader IP Multimedia Subsystem. It is also marketed as Advanced Messaging, Chat, joyn, SMSoIP, Message+, and SMS+. — Wikipedia

In China, the telecom operators call the RCS a 5G Chatbot. (See: Chinese operators make major RCS commitment: Whitepaper, April 9, 2020)
